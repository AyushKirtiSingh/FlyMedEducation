FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is MBBS from abroad valid in India? +
Yes. MBBS degrees from NMC and WHO approved universities are valid in India. After completing your degree, you simply need to clear the FMGE (Foreign Medical Graduate Examination) to practice medicine in India.
What is the total cost of MBBS abroad? +
The total cost including tuition fees, hostel, and other expenses ranges from ₹30 to ₹40 Lakhs for the full MBBS program. This is significantly lower than private MBBS colleges in India which can cost ₹60 Lakhs to ₹1 Crore or more.
What is the eligibility to apply for MBBS abroad? +
You need to have completed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology with at least 50% marks. You also need a valid NEET score. Our counselors will assess your profile and recommend the best university for your eligibility.
Is it safe to study abroad? +
Absolutely. The countries we work with — Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Uzbekistan, Russia, and Georgia — all have established Indian student communities, active Indian embassies, and student-friendly environments. FlyMed Abroad also provides pre-departure briefings and on-ground support.
How does FlyMed Abroad help with visa processing? +
We provide 100% visa assistance. Our team prepares all documentation, guides you through the application process, liaises with embassies, and ensures your student visa is obtained smoothly and on time before your course begins.
Are the courses taught in English? +
Yes. All medical programs at our partner universities are offered in English medium, so you can focus entirely on your studies without any language barrier. Some universities offer optional local language classes to help with daily life.
